# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Create an e-commerce website for KanhaRas Spices to sell various spices online with product catalog, shopping cart, and checkout functionality.

**Planned changes:**
- Build product catalog page displaying spices with images, names, prices, and descriptions in a grid layout with search/filter capabilities
- Create individual product detail pages with complete information and add-to-cart functionality
- Implement shopping cart system allowing customers to add items, adjust quantities, and view totals
- Build checkout flow collecting shipping information (name, address, phone, email) and displaying order summary with validation
- Design warm, inviting visual theme with earth tones and elegant typography reflecting traditional spice markets
- Display "KanhaRas Spices" brand name prominently in header across all pages
- Store and retrieve product data from backend

**User-visible outcome:** Customers can browse a catalog of spices, view detailed product information, add items to their cart, adjust quantities, and complete checkout by providing shipping details and reviewing their order.
